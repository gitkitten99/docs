# Grok

<Callout type="info" emoji="🚀" title="What makes Grok stand out">
It has a sense of humor—and a direct pipeline to the live X (Twitter) firehose for super-fresh takes. :contentReference[oaicite:12]{index=12}  
</Callout>

## Need-to-know facts

- **Latest engine** – Grok-2 packs 314 B parameters and mixes expert “modules” for sharper reasoning. :contentReference[oaicite:13]{index=13}  
- **Big memory** – Early testers report context windows up to 256k tokens, with research into million-token range for Grok 3. :contentReference[oaicite:14]{index=14}  
- **Real-time edge** – It ingests public X posts in near-real time, so it can comment on breaking news minutes after it happens. :contentReference[oaicite:15]{index=15}  

## Everyday fun

- “Summarize the last hour of WWDC tweets.”  
- “Give me a spicy but safe joke about procrastination.”  
- “Draft a meme caption for this photo.”  

> Grok’s playful tone can edge into snark—great for social media, less so for formal emails.
